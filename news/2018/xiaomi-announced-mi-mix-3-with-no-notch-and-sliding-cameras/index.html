Xiaomi Announced Mi Mix 3 with no notch and sliding cameras

  •  By  Maung Thuu
  • On  Oct 25, 2018
  • In  News
Facebook Twitter

The year 2018 is full of fullscreen and notch trends in the mobile phone market. Today, Xioami announced Mi Mix 3 with no notch and sliding cameras. Yes, Xioami finally has smartphones with sliding cameras like Oppo Find X.

Xiaomi claims a screen-to-body ratio of 93.4 percent (increased 3.82%) and has cut down on the “chin” at the bottom of the display by nearly 4.5 millimeters compared to the Mi Mix 2S.

Mi Mix 3 comes with 6.39″ AMOLED Full HD Display. Thanks to AMOLED Display, there will be Always On feature. There are four total cameras: 12-megapixel wide-angle and telephoto modules on the back, and a 24-megapixel selfie camera backed by a 2-megapixel sensor on the front. Unlike Oppo Find X, there is a fingerprint sensor on the back. Users don’t need to open slide camera everytime they try to unlock the device. Slow-motion video can be recorded with 960 fps.

Also, Mix 3 beats their company’s devices Mi MIX 2S & Mi 8 in DxO Mark Score.

Every other flagship phones come with  Snapdragon 845 processor this year, Mi Mix 3  has with Snapdragon 845 but come with the memory of up to 10GB. That’s insane, my laptop has only 4GB of RAM. Mi Mix 3 also has the capability of 5G Network and wireless fast charging.

There will be 3 colors and Forbidden City Palace Museum Edition.

The Mi Mix 3 will be out on November 1st in China, starting at 3,299 yuan ($475) for a model with 6GB of RAM and 128GB of storage; that’s the same base price that the 64GB Mi 2S launched at. 3,999 yuan ($575) gets you 8GB of RAM and 256GB of storage. The 10GB model is a “Forbidden City Palace Museum” special edition variant that comes with 256GB of storage and costs 4,999 yuan ($720). Pricing for India and other markets should be announced soon.

 

More information can be found on MIUI Forum.
